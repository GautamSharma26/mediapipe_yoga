Tree Pose (Vrksasana)
1.    Stand tall with your feet together and arms at your sides.
2.    Shift your weight onto your left foot.
3.    Bend your right knee and place the sole of your right foot on your inner left thigh or calf (avoid the knee).
4.    Bring your hands together in a prayer position at your chest.
5.    Find a focal point in front of you to help with balance.
6.    Hold for 1-2 minutes, then switch sides.

Vajrasana (Thunderbolt Pose)
1.    Kneel on the floor with your knees together and feet slightly apart.
2.    Sit back on your heels, keeping your spine straight.
3.    Place your hands on your thighs with palms facing down.
4.    Close your eyes and breathe deeply.
5.    Hold for 1-5 minutes, focusing on your breath.

Warrior II Pose (Virabhadrasana II)
1.    Stand with your feet wide apart (about 3-4 feet).
2.    Turn your right foot out 90 degrees and your left foot slightly in.
3.    Raise your arms parallel to the floor with palms facing down.
4.    Bend your right knee over your right ankle, keeping the thigh parallel to the floor.
5.    Gaze over your right hand.
6.    Hold for 30 seconds to 1 minute, then switch sides.

T Pose (Tadasana Variation)
1.    Stand tall with your feet together and arms at your sides.
2.    Shift your weight onto your left foot.
3.    Lift your right leg straight out behind you, keeping it parallel to the floor.
4.    Extend your arms straight out to the sides to form a "T" shape.
5.    Keep your hips square and your gaze focused on a point in front of you.
6.    Hold for 30 seconds to 1 minute, then switch sides.
